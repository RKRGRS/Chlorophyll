Chlorophyll was first isolated and named byJoseph Bienaime` Caventou and pierre Joseph Pelletier in 1817.Chlophyll is the green pigment located in the Chloroplast that are the site for the process of photosynthesis.The Chlorophyll pigment is responsible for the green colouration in plants.The name Chlorophyll is derived from two greek words ; chloros (green) and phyllon (leaf).The empirical formula of chlorophyll is C55H72 O5N4Mg.
